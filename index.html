Push Notification

To see in action. Follow the steps :

  • Click on subscribe button. On the bottom right of the page.
  • You will be asked to allow / block notification. Click on "allow".
  • You would now see a bell icon replacing the "subscribe" button
  • Now click the bell icon.
  • A popover would appear with the subscription object. Copy it all.
  • Open this link
  • Paste in the "Subscription to Send To" section
  • Type in your message you want
  • Now click "Send message" and see a popup should come.
